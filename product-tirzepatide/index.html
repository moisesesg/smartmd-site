What is GLP-2TZ?

GLP-2TZ is a dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist — a next-generation incretin-pathway compound that simultaneously activates both glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide (GIP) and glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptors. This dual mechanism distinguishes it from single-target GLP-1 agonists like glp-1sm.

With 100+ published references in the preclinical and clinical literature, glp-2tz is one of the most actively studied metabolic peptides. Researchers examine its role in dual incretin pathway activation, glucose-dependent insulin signaling, appetite regulation, and broader metabolic pathway modulation.

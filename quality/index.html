The SMART MD Standard

7 Steps. Every Batch.
No Exceptions.

This is what we mean by verification. Not a marketing claim — a documented, repeatable process that runs on every batch without exception.

01

GMP Synthesis Sourcing

Every peptide originates from a verified synthesis partner under Good Manufacturing Practice conditions with documented chain-of-custody records. We do not purchase from anonymous or unverified sources.

02

Internal QC Gate

Visual inspection, lyophilization quality assessment, vial integrity, packaging check. If it doesn't clear this gate on arrival, it never reaches an external lab. Low tolerance for anything that looks off.

03

HPLC — Lab 1

High-performance liquid chromatography at an accredited, independent external facility. Measures peptide purity by separating and quantifying all components in the sample.

✓ Hard reject below 98.0% purity
04

Mass Spectrometry — Lab 2

Independent molecular weight confirmation at a second facility. Rules out structural analogues that can pass HPLC but have different molecular identity. Not optional — it's the identity check HPLC cannot perform.

✓ Molecular weight must match theoretical
05

Endotoxin + Microbial — Lab 3

LAL (Limulus Amebocyte Lysate) endotoxin assay and full microbial panel at a third, completely independent facility. The step most research peptide suppliers skip because it adds cost and time. We consider it non-negotiable.

✓ Hard reject above 5 EU/mg endotoxin
06

COA Issued + Batch Logged

Certificate of Analysis documents issued by all three labs are compiled into a single batch record. Batch number assigned and permanently logged to our public verification database. The COA is live and searchable from this point forward — forever.

07

Released to Researchers

Only after all three labs pass on all metrics. This adds 5–10 days to turnaround versus suppliers who ship on single-lab results. That's the correct tradeoff. We've never reconsidered it.

✓ Verifiable at any time, by anyone, for free

7 Steps. Every Batch.
No Exceptions.

01

GMP Synthesis Sourcing

Verified partners under Good Manufacturing Practice conditions with documented chain-of-custody records.

02

Internal QC Gate

Visual inspection, lyophilization quality, packaging integrity. Doesn't clear this gate, never reaches an external lab.

03

HPLC — Lab 1

High-performance liquid chromatography. Purity confirmation at accredited external facility.

04

Mass Spectrometry — Lab 2

Independent molecular weight verification. Rules out structural analogues that HPLC can't detect.

05

Endotoxin + Microbial — Lab 3

LAL endotoxin assay + full microbial panel at a third independent facility. The step most suppliers skip entirely.

Must pass <5 EU/mg
06

07

Where SMART MD wins

  • Verification depth. 3 independent U.S. labs vs Limitless Biotech's 2-source model (manufacturer + Janoshik). For research where you need to defend the analytical chain, more independent verification is strictly better.

Where Limitless Biotech wins

  • Catalog age + brand recognition. Limitless Biotech has a longer market history and broader name recognition.
  • Catalog breadth in some research areas. Some niche compounds may be in stock at Limitless Biotech that SMART MD has not yet sourced.

Bottom line

If verification depth, pre-purchase COA verifiability, direct phone support, or a structured wholesale program for clinics matter to your research, SMART MD has the stronger offering on those specific dimensions. If catalog breadth or longer brand history is the deciding factor, Limitless Biotech is the established alternative.

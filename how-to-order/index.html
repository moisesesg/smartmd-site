HOW TO ORDER

First time? Here's everything you need to know.

1

Browse compounds

Visit our full compound catalog to explore all available research peptides. Each product page includes research context, purity data, and COA verification links.

2

Click "Buy Now"

On any product page, click the "Buy Now" button. You'll land on our secure store at store.smartmdpeptides.com where you can review product details and pricing.

3

Add to cart & enter shipping info

Add your selected compounds to your cart. Enter your shipping address at checkout. We ship to all 50 U.S. states.

4

Pay securely via Stripe

All payments are processed through Stripe — industry-standard encryption. Accepted: credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

5

Order confirmation + tracking

You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately. When your order ships, you'll get a tracking number via email so you can follow your package in real time.

6

Verify your COA

When your shipment arrives, verify your lot-specific Certificate of Analysis at smartmdpeptides.com/verify. Every batch is independently tested by 3 U.S. laboratories.

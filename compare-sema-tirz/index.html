Research comparison FAQ

GLP-1SM is a single GLP-1 receptor agonist — it activates one incretin pathway. GLP-2TZ is a dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist — it activates both incretin pathways simultaneously. In preclinical models, the dual-agonist mechanism is studied for potentially stronger glucose-dependent insulin signaling and metabolic pathway modulation compared to single-agonist approaches. Both are classified as research-use-only and are available at SMART MD Peptides with 3-lab COA verification.

GLP-1SM has a larger published base (200+ studies vs 100+ for GLP-2TZ), primarily because it entered research earlier. GLP-2TZ's published literature is growing rapidly as dual-agonist research expands. Both have extensive preclinical and clinical-context publications, though SMART MD supplies the research-grade compound classification only.

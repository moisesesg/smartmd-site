The SMART MD Standard

7 Steps. Every Batch.
No Exceptions.

01

GMP Synthesis Sourcing

Every peptide originates from a verified partner under Good Manufacturing Practice conditions with documented chain-of-custody records.

02

Internal QC Gate

Visual inspection, lyophilization quality, packaging integrity. Doesn't clear this gate, never reaches an external lab.

03

HPLC — Lab 1

High-performance liquid chromatography. Purity confirmation at accredited external facility.

✓ Hard reject below 98.0%
04

Mass Spectrometry — Lab 2

Independent molecular weight verification. Rules out structural analogues that HPLC can't detect.

05

Endotoxin + Microbial — Lab 3

LAL endotoxin assay + full microbial panel at a third independent facility. The step most suppliers skip entirely.

✓ Must pass <5 EU/mg
06

COA Issued + Batch Logged

Certificate of Analysis from all three labs. Batch number permanently logged to our public verification system.

07

Released to Researchers

Only after all three labs pass. Adds time. That's the correct tradeoff and we've never reconsidered it.
